Data Availability Statement

The source code and data used in this study are publicly available at https://github.com/junseong2im/UMN.

The Snitz et al. (2013) mixture similarity dataset is publicly available through the DREAM Olfaction Challenge (https://www.dreamchallenges.org/).

The Ravia et al. (2020) dataset is available in the supplementary materials of the original publication.

All experimental results reported in this paper are reproducible using the scripts provided in the repository.
